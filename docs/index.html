Orazio Gallo

NVIDIA Research

I am a Principal Research Scientist and Senior Research Manager at NVIDIA Research, which I joined in 2011. I am interested in computational photography and low-level computer vision particularly for autonomous navigation and robotics applications.

I am a senior associate editor of the IEEE Transactions on Computational Imaging. From 2018 to 2022 I was a member of the IEEE Computational Imaging Technical Committee, and from 2015 to 2017 I was an associate editor for Signal processing: Image Communication.

Want to join?

If you have similar research interests including, but not limited to anything 3D (e.g., monocular/multi-view depth estimation, SLAM, SfM, etc.), anything optical/scene flow, anything novel view synthesis, please reach out. We have internships (for PhD students) and full-time positions. Shoot me an email!
